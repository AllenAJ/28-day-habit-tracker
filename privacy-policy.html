Privacy Policy – 28 Days Habit Tracker

Last updated: April 2026

28 Days Habit Tracker (“the extension”) helps you track daily habits in Chrome. It can optionally use the bundled ZeroGPU Browser SDK for habit-related AI features (for example when the product surfaces summarization or classification tied to your habits). That optional AI path is part of the same product purpose as habit tracking.

Habit data stored locally

  • The extension stores habit names and which days you marked habits complete.
  • This data is kept in your browser using Chrome’s extension storage (chrome.storage.sync). It may sync across your devices if you use Chrome Sync; that sync is handled by Chrome.

Optional AI (ZeroGPU)

When you use features that rely on the ZeroGPU SDK, the extension may send habit-related text or other content needed for that feature to ZeroGPU’s services over HTTPS and WebSocket (for example to run inference or orchestration). The developer does not use this for unrelated advertising or profiling.

If you do not use ZeroGPU-dependent features, habit data stays in your browser as described above.

Data we do not collect as the developer

We do not collect, sell, or use your data for:

  • Creditworthiness or lending decisions
  • General web browsing history or page content from websites you visit (the extension does not inject scripts into pages for reading site content)
  • Analytics unrelated to operating the extension
