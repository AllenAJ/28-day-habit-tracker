Privacy Policy – ZeroGPU Habit Tracker

Last updated: March 2025

ZeroGPU Habit Tracker ("the extension") is designed to respect your privacy.

Data storage

  • The extension stores only the data you create in it: habit names and which days you marked them complete.
  • This data is kept in your browser using Chrome's extension storage (chrome.storage.sync). It may sync across your devices if you use Chrome Sync; that sync is handled by Chrome, not by the extension or its developer.
  • No habit data is sent to any external server or third party.

Data we do not collect

We do not collect, receive, or have access to:

  • Personally identifiable information (name, email, etc.)
  • Health, financial, or authentication information
  • Location, web history, or website content
  • Any analytics or usage data from the extension
